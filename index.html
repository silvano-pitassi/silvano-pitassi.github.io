My research activities focus on the analysis of numerical schemes on generic polytopal meshes for a wide range of electromagnetic models. I develop discrete functional analysis tools and efficient numerical algorithms applicable to low- and high-order Mimetic Finite Difference methods, Hybrid High-Order methods, and discretizations based on discrete complexes, such as Finite Element Exterior Calculus and Discrete De Rham methods.
